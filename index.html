Top 5 Most Popular Pokémon

Pikachu

Pikachu is an Electric-type Pokémon and is the mascot of the Pokémon franchise.

Charizard

Charizard is a Fire/Flying-type Pokémon and is known for its powerful attacks and fierce demeanor.

Bulbasaur

Bulbasaur is a Grass/Poison-type Pokémon and is one of the original starter Pokémon.

Squirtle

Squirtle is a Water-type Pokémon and is known for its ability to shoot water from its mouth.

Eevee

Eevee is a Normal-type Pokémon and is known for its ability to evolve into various forms.
